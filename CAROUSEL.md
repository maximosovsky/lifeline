## Slide 2 — How it works

### How it works

📏 Set your range — how many years back and forward
📝 Add events — one line each:
```
3, Product launch, 2018
4, 1979-1990, School, lightblue
```
🎓 Toggle milestones — Eurostat life stage statistics
📄 Export — SVG or PDF with embedded fonts

Runs in your browser. No signup. No server.
