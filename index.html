WallPlan — Calendar Generator for Long-Term Planning

Every year we publish a new version of a multi-year calendar. The publishing industry mostly makes calendars for just the next year, limiting our ability to plan in detail many years ahead. WallPlan is a free online tool that generates multi-year wall calendars with Gantt charts for strategic planning.

Features

SVG Renderer: Pure SVG output with three calendar types — Vertical (classic days list), Gantt (horizontal planning grid), and Box (traditional mini month grid).

Duration from 1 month to 20 years. Mobile scroll-wheel picker for years and months, desktop number input.

Hide Days Mode: Toggle to hide day-level details from Vertical and Gantt views. Gantt rows redistribute to fill full height — ideal for long-term strategic planning. Persisted via URL parameter.

Paper Sizes: A4, A3, 914mm single roll, 914mm ×2 dual copies, 914mm ×4 quad copies. Precision layout automatically fits 7–8 months per page.

Format-Specific Tuning: Each roll format has optimized cell widths — 4mm single, 2.7mm dual, 1.7mm quad — and font scaling for readability at each physical size.

Export: Download as vector SVG or PDF with embedded IBM Plex Sans fonts. Free, no login required.

Customizable: Week start Monday or Sunday, US Federal Holidays, adjustable Gantt rows 6, 8, 10, or 12.

Custom Entries: Add session-only annotations via the T button. Supports DD.MM date input with auto-formatting, yearly repeat option. Entries render alongside holidays and export to SVG and PDF.

WallPlan Day: January 11 — marked annually in Copper Penny DTP font with wine-colored accent.

Social Preview: Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags with app screenshot for rich link previews in Telegram, Facebook, Twitter.

Printing

Long multi-year calendars can be printed at copy centers on engineering paper up to 1 meter wide with virtually infinite length. Ideal for working on strategies. Print optimized — what you see is what you get.

Mobile

Touch pan and pinch-to-zoom. iOS-like bottom toolbar with frosted glass effect shows current duration, rows count, and paper format. Tap to open settings sheet with scroll-wheel duration picker, paper format selector with roll length display, Gantt rows chips, and week start toggle. Download icon opens SVG and PDF popup. T button for custom entries. Hide Days toggle.

Technical

Rulers and guides with cm/m labels, vertical guides marking printable area. Batch SVG path rendering, holiday caching, requestAnimationFrame-based viewport updates. Russian edition available with Russian holidays. Google Analytics GA4 tracking.
